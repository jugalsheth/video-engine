ZOOM_HOOK
  When: Always, first 0.3 seconds — NON-NEGOTIABLE
  Params: snap_frames (6), hold_frames (6), ease_frames (20), peak_scale (1.18), settle_scale (1.08)
  Total: 32 frames — snap → hold → ease back

TITLE_CARD
  When: Frame 0, simultaneous with hook snap
  Params: text, subtitle, animation (slam_up)
  Duration: minimum 120 frames (4 seconds at 30fps)
  Style: heaviest weight, 80% screen width, all caps

CAPTION_HIGHLIGHT
  When: Full video duration
  Params: auto (true) — driven by word timestamps

STAT_CALLOUT
  When: Exact frame a stat number is spoken
  Params: number, label, position (left/right)

STEP_REVEAL
  When: Exact frame creator says One/Two/Three
  Params: step_number, text

WORD_HIGHLIGHT
  When: Energy words — Right, Unless, That's all
  Params: word, color (cyan), style (flash)

BROLL_OVERLAY (stock video — auto-detected)
  When: Keyword matches broll_detector types (data_flow, terminal, salary, etc.)
  Pipeline fetches portrait stock clips from Pexels (primary) / Pixabay (fallback)
  Rate limit: max 1 B-roll per 8 seconds of video
  Remotion: StockVideoBroll with SVG fallback if clip missing
  Params: type, start_frame, end_frame, search_query, clip_file

ZOOM_CLOSER
  When: Final 3 seconds
  Params: start_scale (1.08), end_scale (1.12)

ROLE_CAST (auto-detected — reaction characters)
  Pipeline: role_caster.py → role_moments.json → RoleRouter.tsx
  Cast: victim (pain/fail), hype (wins), skeptic (doubt), expert (tech), gremlin (chaos)
  Each role: SVG character + comic speech bubble + pose animation + callback support
  mood medium: 1 role per 8s | chaos: 1 per 5s + gremlin + manga lines

FUN_MOMENTS (atmosphere FX only — mood: medium | chaos)
  Pipeline: fun_detector.py → fun_moments.json → FunRouter.tsx
  medium mood: comic_sfx, emoji_pop, speech_bubble, doodle_arrow, confetti, mind_blown, red_x, question_bounce (max 1 per 8s)
  chaos mood: all medium types + money_rain, fire_spark, manga_lines (max 1 per 5s)
  SVG: ComicSFX, EmojiPop, DoodleArrow, SpeechBubble, RedXSlam, QuestionBounce, MangaLines
  Lottie: confetti, money_rain, fire_spark, mind_blown in public/lottie/fun/
