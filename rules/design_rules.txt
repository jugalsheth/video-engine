ANIMATION PHILOSOPHY (read this before every decision):
Subtle is the enemy. This content competes with thousands of videos.
Every animation choice should feel intentional and slightly aggressive.
The viewer's thumb is moving. You have 0.3 seconds to stop it.
If an animation could be described as "gentle" — make it stronger.
If a callout could be described as "clean" — make it bolder.
The brand is dark, editorial, and confident — not soft or corporate.
Energy without chaos. Bold without overwhelming.

BRAND IDENTITY:
Background: Always dark (#0C0B09 obsidian)
Primary accent: Cyan (#00D4FF) for emphasis and captions
Secondary accent: Amber (#C9923A) for stats and numbers
Title font: Bold sans-serif, heaviest weight, all caps
Caption font: Clean sans-serif, minimum 36px
Never use: white backgrounds, rounded cards, bright colors
Never cover the creator's face with any graphic element

HOOK ANIMATION (first 0.3 seconds — non-negotiable):
Snap zoom: scale 1.0 → 1.18 in exactly 6 frames (aggressive, sharp)
Hold zoom at 1.18 for 6 frames
Ease back to 1.08 over 20 frames
This is the single most important retention moment in the video
Do NOT make this subtle — it must be jarring enough to interrupt scrolling

TITLE CARD (simultaneous with hook snap):
Title text: SLAM in from bottom, not drop from top
Title appears simultaneously with zoom snap (frame 0)
Font weight: heaviest possible, all caps, fills 80% of screen width
Title stays for minimum 4 seconds (120 frames at 30fps)
Subtitle: cyan, bold, appears with title

CAPTION STYLE (VIRAL / HORMOZI MODE — default):
Always on — never off
Max 2 words on screen at once — never more
All caps, font weight 900, 52px minimum
Active word: amber highlight (#C9923A), scale 1.15 spring
Inactive word: #F5F0E8 at 85% opacity
Heavy black text shadow for readability — no background strip in viral mode
Position: 75% from top (above nav, below chin)
SFX: subtle tick on energy words

STAT CALLOUTS:
Trigger: exact frame when stat number is spoken
Animation: count-up from 0 over 30 frames (fast, punchy)
Color: amber (#C9923A) for number, white for label
Size: large — scroll-stopping, never timid
Position: right side, never covering face
Hold visible: minimum 3 seconds after count-up

STEP REVEALS:
Always sequential — never simultaneous
Each step: hard slide from left with spring overshoot
Step number: cyan circle with white number, bold
Previous steps stay visible when next appears
Trigger: exact frame creator says One/Two/Three

B-ROLL OVERLAYS:
Duration: 2-3 seconds max
Position: top right corner, 28% frame width max
Animation: fast fade in and fade out
Never during stat or step moments

CLOSER (final 3 seconds):
Zoom in: 1.08 to 1.12
Captions slightly larger
No additional graphics — quiet and human

ENERGY WORDS (Right, Unless, That's all, Listen):
Sharp cyan flash on the word in captions — visible, not subtle

NEVER:
More than 2 graphic elements simultaneously
Any overlay covering the creator's face
Rounded pill badges or card backgrounds
Cheerful or bright color schemes
Gentle, soft, or corporate-feeling animations
